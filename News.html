Over View

A new hero has arrived in Overwatch 2. Kiriko a new support character trained by the shimada clan is here. Kiriko uses her training as a ninja to move fast and get to teammates fast and her connections with the fox spirit to heal and buff her teammates, using her will bring a huge impact to your team.

Congratulations to Dallas Fuel for winning the grand finals of the Overwatch League against San Fransisco Shock.
